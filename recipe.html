Quinoa Tabbouleh

A Middle Eastern salad - perfect for summer!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 large english cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 2/3 cup parsley
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint
  • 2 scallions

Directions

Boil the quinoa according to directions. Fluff with a fork. Let cool. Whisk the lemon juice and garlic in a small bowl. Gradually whisk in olive oil. Season with salt and pepper. Once quinoa is cooled, add dressing and remaining ingredients. Toss to coat.
